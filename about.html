BuuPass is driven by young entrepreneurs who are passionate about solving real everyday problems by making simple and powerful solutions. Their idea was born in response to the 2016 Hult Prize Challenge (www.hultprize.com) a global student competition for social entrepreneurship, which launches students into developing scalable business models in order to solve global challenges. The challenge for 2016 was to better connect people to goods, services, and resources. After a year long process, the diverse team went on to win the prestigious competition with President Bill Clinton, under their former name Magic Bus Ticketing. The BuuPass team is motivated by the idea of transforming inefficient transport systems into a catalyst for growth and impact, in order to connect people to opportunities.

GENERAL QUESTION

  1. Advantages of booking with buupass?

    -  You can book your ticket from anywhere anytime using any device.

    -  Saves you time and money.

    -  Provides you with a variety of bus options

    -  You can select your seat and pay via Mpesa.

  2. Does booking online cost more?

    No,booking online via Buupass charge the same as over the counter.

  3. What is buupass?

TICKET RELATED

  1. How to Book

    Dial *877# from your safaricom line or visit www.buupass.com for bookings.

  2. Ticket Alteration/Cancelling?

    This can be done from respective bus company.

  3. Resending ticket/lost ticket

    In case of lose or no confirmation message received kindly contact our customer care for assistance.

  4. Printing/Where I get a ticket?

    Tickets are printed from the bus station after presentation of the confirmation SMS.

  5. Booked multiple seats but only one name appears on the confirmation SMS?

    Kindly check if the number of seats captured in the ticket SMS matches the tickets paid for.

PAYMENT RELATED

  1. What payment options do I have?

    Currently Mpesa is the only available payment option.

  2. Did not receive any mpesa pop up prompting the payment ?

    If a pop up does not appear on your phone a text message follows soon with the details on how to make payments via paybill

    Also for the pop up to appear a client should update their mpesa menu.

  3. Payment Timeout?

    It will take less than 4min to make payments

    Longer than 4min the client has to do the booking once again to receive another valid account number to be able to make payments.
